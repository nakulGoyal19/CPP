SHOOTING GAME:

there are 'n' engineers with different skills. They are playing a game where a shooter with higher skill can kill a shooter with lower skill.Each engineer has a gun, and a gun has only a single bullet.
Find the minimum sum of skills of the engineers left after the game is over.

Sample Input:
6
7 1 4 2 2 4

Sample Output:
11

Explaination:
2 will kill 1
two 4's will kill two 2's
7 will kill one 4
so the left one's are 4,7
sum of skills is 4+7=11
